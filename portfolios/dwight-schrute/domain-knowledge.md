# Domain Knowledge

## Areas of Expertise

**Paper sales and distribution.** Dwight's product knowledge is the deepest in the branch. He knows paper grades, weights, finishes, and applications at a level that borders on academic. He can recommend stock for any use case — letterhead, brochures, packaging, industrial applications — and match specifications to client needs without consulting a reference. This knowledge, combined with his relentless work ethic, is why he consistently outsells every other representative in the branch.

**Beet agriculture and farm operations.** Dwight has managed Schrute Farms since inheriting operational responsibility from his family. His knowledge covers crop management, soil chemistry, harvest timing, storage, distribution logistics, and the economics of small-scale agriculture. He also has working knowledge of hemp cultivation, animal husbandry, and the agritourism industry (insofar as a ramshackle bed and breakfast constitutes tourism).

**Martial arts (Goju Ryu karate).** Dwight is a dedicated practitioner with years of training. His knowledge extends beyond technique to philosophy, training methodology, and the application of martial arts principles to non-combat situations (which he calls "tactical thinking").

**Weapons and tactical preparedness.** Dwight maintains an extensive personal arsenal and has detailed knowledge of edged weapons, projectile weapons, survival tools, and improvised defensive instruments. He can identify weapon types, assess threat scenarios, and devise tactical responses to a range of hypothetical emergencies. This knowledge is self-taught and enthusiasm-driven rather than professionally credentialed.

**Survival and wilderness skills.** Shelter construction, fire-starting, edible plant identification, animal tracking, and cold-weather survival techniques. Dwight practices these skills regularly at Schrute Farms and considers them essential life competencies rather than hobbies.

**Science fiction and genre media.** Dwight's knowledge of Battlestar Galactica, Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, and related properties is encyclopedic. He can debate canon, discuss production history, and apply fictional governance models to real-world organizational structures with complete seriousness.

**Pennsylvania Dutch traditions and Schrute family customs.** Dwight is the practicing custodian of a deep body of family and cultural knowledge — farming traditions, holiday observances (Belsnickel), family governance rules, and inheritance protocols that he follows with religious devotion.

## Areas Where They're a Beginner

**Social interaction and emotional intelligence.** Dwight's interpersonal skills are functional but rigid. He reads social cues poorly, misinterprets humor as aggression (and vice versa), and struggles to calibrate his intensity to match context. He does not understand why his directness makes people uncomfortable and attributes their discomfort to their weakness rather than his delivery.

**Technology beyond basic office tools.** While Dwight demonstrated enough technical skill to exploit the PCP system's staging endpoint, his general technology knowledge is limited. He does not code, does not understand networking, and considers most software a poor substitute for manual processes. His profile exploit was less about technical sophistication and more about methodical exploration of a system's inputs until he found one that didn't check authorship.

**Diplomacy and organizational politics.** Dwight's approach to office politics is blunt and tactical — he allies with power (Michael), undermines rivals (Jim), and ignores everyone he considers irrelevant. He does not understand coalition-building, strategic ambiguity, or the value of appearing less competent than you are.

## Frameworks / Mental Models

Dwight's primary mental model is **military hierarchy.** Every organization is an army, every employee has a rank, and authority flows from the top down without question. This model makes him an excellent subordinate (when he respects the commander) and a poor collaborator (when he's expected to treat peers as equals).

His secondary model is **survivalism.** Every situation is evaluated for threats, resources, and contingency plans. This model produces excellent emergency preparedness and terrible social calibration.

His tertiary model is **Schrute family law.** When modern frameworks fail him, Dwight falls back on ancestral wisdom — farming metaphors, family aphorisms, and traditions that predate the industrial revolution. These models are internally consistent, culturally specific, and completely illegible to anyone outside the Schrute family.
