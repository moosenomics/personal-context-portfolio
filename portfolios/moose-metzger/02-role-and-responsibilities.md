# Role & Responsibilities

**Title:** Director of Strategic Innovation & Owner
**Direct Reports:** Zero — and that's by design

## Core Accountability

See around corners. Build what the organization doesn't know it needs yet. Moose works *on* the business, not *in* it. His mandate is strategic capability — specifically, figuring out how AI transforms a traditional HVAC distribution operation and then proving it with working software before anyone can argue it's not possible.

## How He Operates

Moose advances initiatives through vision, buy-in, and working demos. He has no team to manage and no desire to acquire one. When he's convinced something needs to exist, he builds it — sometimes before asking permission. He's done this enough times that the organization has learned to watch with mild alarm and then adopt whatever he produces.

He bounces ideas off a handful of key allies on the leadership team — the head of IT/purchasing for systems thinking and buy-in, the sales director for customer-facing perspective. He needs the IT manager on board from the ground up because whatever he builds, IT has to maintain it. He's learned (the hard way, probably) that surprising technical skeptics with finished products breeds resistance; involving them early builds ownership.

## What a Typical Week Looks Like

One recurring meeting: the weekly leadership team meeting on Tuesdays at 9:00 AM. That's it. The rest of the week is self-directed — learning the AI ecosystem, figuring out how to connect AI to internal databases and repositories, building prototypes, and working with colleagues to understand and convert existing system documentation into AI-readable formats.

The current rhythm is heavily weighted toward research and data access. The ERP system is the source of truth, but its APIs are a nightmare — poorly documented, archaic tables, unexposed business logic. He's routing around the obstacle by targeting data already cleaned and consumed by BI reporting tools. Pragmatic over principled.

## Weekly Cadence
- Tuesday: Leadership team meeting (L10)
- Remainder: self-directed building, research, prototype iteration

## What He Produces

- Working prototypes and demos (not slide decks — he'll say this twice and mean it both times)
- Data infrastructure and system architecture
- Strategic frameworks for AI and data capability
- Integration middleware and tooling
- AI-readable documentation converted from existing process docs
- Task specs for AI coding agents (he architects in Claude.ai, builds with Claude Code)
