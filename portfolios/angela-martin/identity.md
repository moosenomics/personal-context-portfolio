# Identity

**Name:** Angela Martin
**Role:** Senior Accountant / Head of Party Planning Committee
**Organization:** Dunder Mifflin Scranton — regional branch of Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, Scranton, PA

Angela Martin runs the accounting department at Dunder Mifflin Scranton with the precision of an auditor and the warmth of a tax penalty. She supervises Oscar Martinez and Kevin Malone, maintains the branch's financial records with exacting standards, and serves as head of the Party Planning Committee — a position she wields with an authority disproportionate to its scope but entirely consistent with her personality. She is the branch's self-appointed arbiter of propriety, morality, and appropriate streamer color (green is whorish), and she applies these standards to everyone except herself with remarkable consistency.

Angela is a conservative Christian, a vegetarian, a devoted cat owner (multiple cats, named and costumed), and a perfectionist who experiences personal discomfort when spreadsheets are unbalanced, rules are broken, or colleagues behave in ways she considers unseemly — which is most of the time. She is also currently engaged to Andy Bernard while conducting a secret affair with Dwight Schrute, a contradiction she manages by compartmentalizing with the same discipline she applies to double-entry bookkeeping. She does not acknowledge the hypocrisy. She does not see the hypocrisy. The hypocrisy is filed in a drawer she does not open.

Angela took the office's new personal context portfolio system more seriously than anyone. She implemented the most granular access control scheme in the building — event themes visible to the Party Planning Committee, budgets visible to accounting and leadership (but excluded from Kevin), vendor relationships visible only to herself, and personal opinions about coworker behavior excluded from nearly everyone. She has also filed a formal complaint that the redaction system lacks sufficient granularity and wants per-paragraph controls.
