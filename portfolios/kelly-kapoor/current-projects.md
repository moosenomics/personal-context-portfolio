# Current Projects

## Customer Service Excellence (Ongoing)
- **What:** Handling all inbound customer complaints for the Scranton branch, maintaining service quality, and escalating unresolved issues to the appropriate salesperson.
- **Status:** Active, perpetual
- **Kelly's role:** Sole operator
- **Key detail:** Kelly processes an average of fifteen to twenty-five customer interactions per day, ranging from simple order inquiries to heated complaints about delivery failures. She has developed a personal technique that involves matching the caller's emotional energy, validating their frustration, apologizing with conviction, and then pivoting to resolution — all while sounding like she genuinely cares, which, for the duration of the call, she does. She occasionally uses fake names or accents when bored, which is unprofessional but hasn't generated complaints.
- **Priority:** Professionally, this is Kelly's entire job. Personally, it ranks below Ryan, celebrity news, and whatever drama is currently unfolding in the main office.
