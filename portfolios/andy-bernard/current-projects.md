# Current Projects

## Q-Series Regional Account Expansion
- **What:** Systematic effort to grow Andy's client portfolio by targeting mid-size businesses in the greater Scranton-Wilkes Barre corridor that currently buy paper from competitors.
- **Status:** Active, slow progress
- **Andy's role:** Lead salesman, sole contributor
- **Key detail:** Andy built a prospect list of forty-two companies, color-coded by industry vertical and "vibe." He has contacted eleven of them. Three returned his calls. One placed a small order. Andy considers this a strong pipeline.
- **Priority:** Officially high. Practically, it gets attention in bursts between wedding planning and social maintenance.

## The Bernard-Martin Wedding
- **What:** Planning Andy's wedding to Angela Martin, currently the most important project in Andy's life by a wide margin.
- **Status:** Active, under significant strain from Angela's exacting requirements
- **Andy's role:** Primary planner (Angela's role is primarily vetoing)
- **Key collaborators:** Angela Martin (fiancée, chief critic), Here Comes Treble (reception entertainment, confirmed)
- **Key detail:** Andy has proposed and lost non-refundable deposits on EPCOT Center, a Marriott ballroom, hot-air balloon rides, and several other venues, all rejected by Angela. The current leading option is Schrute Farms, which Angela selected for reasons Andy hasn't fully examined. He carries a binder of fabric swatches, seating charts, and catering menus in his messenger bag at all times. The wedding cake will be shaped like a sailboat.
- **Priority:** The highest priority in Andy's life. He would cancel client meetings for a cake tasting without hesitation.

- **Andy's role:** Author, editor, self-promoter

## "The Five Families" Inter-Office Alliance
- **What:** Andy's initiative to build relationships with managers of the other businesses in the Scranton Business Park — essentially a social compact for parking, shared resources, and mutual aid.
- **Status:** Dormant but Andy considers it active
